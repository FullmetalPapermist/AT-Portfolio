
NOTE:
- Your time complexities should be expressed in big-O notation and be in
  terms of n, where n is the number of cells in the maze.

============
  solveBfs
============

- Worst case time complexity: O(n) O(n^2) for display

- Explanation: 
  At worst for each node there are 4 edges to go to and the algorithm would
  check at most n nodes (checking all nodes in the maze.) Hence the worst case
  time complexity would be proportional to 5n or O(n).
============
  solveDfs
============

- Worst case time complexity: O(n) O(n^2) for display

- Explanation: Similar to Bfs, there are at most 4 edges to check for each node
and in the worst case all nodes are checked. Therefore the time complexity must
be proportional to 5n or O(n).
